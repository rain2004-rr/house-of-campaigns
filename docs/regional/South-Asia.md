## 📱 Recording Tips for South Asia

### Lighting Considerations
- Best time: 10 AM - 4 PM (natural light)
- Indoor: Use tube lights/LED bulbs
- Avoid direct sunlight (causes overexposure)

### Power Backup
- Keep power bank ready (power cuts common)
- Ensure full battery before starting
- Have backup storage space

### Common Issues & Solutions
| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Phone heating | Take breaks, remove case |
| Storage full | Clear space beforehand |
| Notification interruptions | Enable Do Not Disturb mode |
| Mount loosening | Tighten with extra zip ties |
