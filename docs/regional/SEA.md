## 📱 Recording Tips for SEA

### Climate Considerations
- **Humidity**: Keep phone dry, use silica gel packets
- **Heat**: Avoid direct sun, phone may overheat
- **Rain**: Have indoor backup plan

### Best Recording Times
- Morning: 8 AM - 11 AM (cooler, good light)
- Evening: 4 PM - 6 PM (before sunset)

### Common Issues & Solutions
| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Phone overheating | Use fan, take breaks |
| Humidity fog | Wipe lens, use anti-fog cloth |
| Power outage | Keep power bank charged |
| Storage full | Use cloud backup or clear space |
